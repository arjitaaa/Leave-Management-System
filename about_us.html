Leave Management System

Digitalizing the process for a smarter, eco-friendly future.

Step 1: Digitizing the System

We have moved beyond the traditional paper-based leave applications and introduced a fully digital platform. This smart system allows students to apply for leaves online, track their application status, and receive instant notifications. The goal is to eliminate manual paperwork, reduce approval time, and make the entire leave process seamless, eco-friendly, and accessible from anywhere.

Step 2: Categorized Leave Management

Our system supports multiple types of leave including Casual Leave, Academic Leave, Emergency Leave, and Seasonal Breaks like Summer or Diwali. Each category is clearly defined, allowing students to apply under the correct type based on their situation. This categorization ensures organized tracking, fair usage, and transparent records of student leaves.
